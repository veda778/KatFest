Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013

This Act protects women from sexual harassment at the workplace.

Sexual harassment includes:
- Physical contact and advances
- Demand or request for sexual favors
- Making sexually colored remarks
- Showing pornography
- Any unwelcome physical, verbal or non-verbal conduct

Employers must form an Internal Complaints Committee (ICC).

Women can file complaints within 3 months of the incident.
